The Mission

Most meditation content is either too mystical or too superficial. Rational Z bridges the gap — breaking down the science of mind training, meditation, and awakening with rigor and clarity.

The Approach

Every claim on this site is backed by peer-reviewed research. We cite our sources. We distinguish between what's proven, what's promising, and what's speculation. If we don't know, we say so.

The Author

Zijing is a Tsinghua University graduate (Electronic Engineering & Business Administration), a certified astrologer (Faculty of Astrological Studies, London), and a meditation teacher with over 5 years of experience guiding thousands of students. Her approach: scientific rigor meets ancient wisdom — no faith required.

Why "Rational Z"?

"Rational" because everything here is evidence-based. "Z" because it stands for both Zijing (紫荆) and Zen (禅) — where rationality meets the depths of mind training.
